Projects

The Steel Reserve

The Steel Reserve was created when two former members of Bellingham's premier old-time music group, the Supersonic Business Jets, decided to start a country duo. Finn and his bandmate, friend, and roommate Logan Day perform heartfelt original songs and classic country deep-cuts with the perfect amount of technical flair and authenticity. The combination of the driving rhythm of Finn's right hand and the tender slides and bends of Logan's Steel is sure to get you honkin' tonkin' all over the dance floor until the the rooster crows.

Virginia Rail

Belligham's beloved bluegrass band Virginia Rail is unfortunatly no longer performing. However, former members including Finn now perform in Madrona led by master mandolin and fiddle player Kian Dye.
